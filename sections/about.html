About the Program

Healthier India Program aims to promote scalable health solutions for CMDs through innovation acceleration, digital health solutions, and international collaboration, supported by Atal Innovation Mission, Novo Nordisk Foundation, and FITT IIT Delhi. The program leverages the knowledge, expertise and network of the three organizations to establish a holistic platform providing multi-faceted support to the innovators.

The two-year program comprises a pre-incubation track for student innovators and an accelerator track for startups, offering targeted mentorship, capacity building, and funding support. Emphasizing digital health, diagnostics, and community-level interventions, the initiative also enables international collaboration, particularly with Denmark's innovation ecosystem.

By integrating public health priorities with innovation, the program seeks to strengthen India's health-tech landscape and improve long-term health outcomes in cardiometabolic diseases.
